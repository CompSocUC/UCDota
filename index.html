UC Dota 2

2018 Tournament

What

Presented by CompSoc and UCGG, the UC Dota 2 LAN is a gaming tournament held at the University of Canterbury, where participants will be playing Dota 2 for the glory of being the best Dota 2 team at UC.

When

11th August 2018
Doors open 9am
Tournament starts 10am

Where

Room 105 at Wheki, University of Canterbury (Dovedale Campus)

Activites

Dota 2 LAN Tournament

The main event where 16-24 teams are seeded into a two type tournament; Round Robin & Double Bracket Elimination. After the round robin the top teams from the pools will be split into upper and lower brackets where they will play a single elimination bracket to determine both first and second place of both brackets.

Pool Games are limited to 1 hour. If the game goes over, then it will be a draw.

Elimination Games are limited to 1.5 hours. If the game goes over, the game will be decided on networth.

All games are best of one until the finals which are best of three.

The main event will be held at Wheki, University of Canterbury (Dovedale Campus).

Lunch (Pizza) will be provided to players who opt into the catering option on the sign up page. Spectators and players can order on the day.

Do note that the tournament will be running all day so the final may be held late in the evening.

Raffle

Raffle tickets will be sold during the tournament for a chance to win a variety of awesome prizes. Tickets will be $4 each, or $10 for 3 tickets, and winners will be drawn at 9pm!
