What

Presented by CompSoc and UCGG, the UC Dota 2 LAN is a gaming tournament held at the University of Canterbury, where participants will be playing Dota 2 in a double elimination bracket games.

When

5th August 2017
Doors open 9am
Tournament starts 10am

Where

Room KE07 at Kirkwood, University of Canterbury

Activites

Dota 2 LAN Tournament

The main event where 16-24 teams are seeded into a two type tournament; Round Robin & Elimination. Each team is guaranteed at least 3 games in Round Robin and a game in the Elimination round.

Pool Games are limited to 1 hour. If the game goes over, then it will be a draw.

Elimination Games are limited to 1.5 hours. If the game goes over, the game will be decided on networth.

All games are best of one until the finals which are best of three.

The main event will be held at Kirkwood, University of Canterbury.

Lunch (Pizza) will be provided to players who opt into the catering option on the sign up page. Spectators can order on the day.

Do note that the tournament will be running all day so the final may be held late in the evening.

Raffle

Raffle tickets will be sold during the tournament for a chance to win a variety of awesome prizes. Tickets will be $3 each, or $5 for 3 tickets, and winners will be drawn at 9pm!

Casting

The event will be casted by Zac Neill from Kai Studio

Kai Studios is a New Zealand based casting organisation focussed on providing a professional, polished product maximising the viewer's experience.
Founded by Zac Neill in 2016, Kai Studios arose out of a need to both grow the scene and to create a sports-broadcast feel to all aspects of the production process.
