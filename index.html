What

Presented by CompSoc and UCGG, the Winterlude Dota 2 LAN is a gaming tournament held at the University of Canterbury, where participants will be playing Dota 2 in a double elimination bracket games.

When

23rd July 2016
Doors open 10am
Tournament starts 11am

Where

Room KG07 at Kirkwood, University of Canterbury

Activites

Dota 2 LAN Tournament

The main event where 16 teams are seeded into double elimination bracket games! All games are best of one until the finals which are best of three. The main event will be held at Kirkwood, University of Canterbury.

Red v Blue Games

Alongside the main tournament, 16 teams will be split into 2 as they battle it in red v Blue. Mini games will be feature as extra activities for players who have been eliminated or are waiting for their next game.

Raffle

Raffle tickets will be sold during the tournament for a chance to win a variety of awesome prizes. Tickets will be $3 each, or $5 for 3 tickets, and winners will be drawn at 9pm!

Casting

The event will be casted by Zac Neill from Kai Studio

Kai Studios is a New Zealand based casting organisation focussed on providing a professional, polished product maximising the viewer's experience.
Founded by Zac Neill in 2016, Kai Studios arose out of a need to both grow the scene and to create a sports-broadcast feel to all aspects of the production process.
